As a post-doctoral researcher and adept developer of cutting-edge tools, my fervor lies in the realm of computer science, particularly in AI computing. My research focus centers on computational geophysics, honed during my tenure as a Ph.D. candidate at Zhejiang University, China.

Presently, I am immersed in multifaceted geosciences engineering endeavors at the School of Geosciences and Info-physics, Central South University, Hunan, China. I'm also A visiting researcher at the Laboratory of Geology, Mineral Resources and Energy (LGRME), Félix Houphouët-Boigny University, Abidjan, Côte d'Ivoire. My work encompasses the development of intelligent methodologies, harnessing the power of Machine Learning, to address diverse challenges including deep resource structure detection (e.g., groundwater, tungsten, tin, ...), land subsidence mitigation, and environmental mine monitoring.
